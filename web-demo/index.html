How the FSA Works

State, action, transition, and validation

State

The game uses [M_left, C_left, Boat], where Boat is 1 on the left bank and 0 on the right bank.

Legal Actions

The boat can carry 1 or 2 passengers: 1M, 1C, 1M+1C, 2M, or 2C.

Validation

A bank is safe when missionaries are not outnumbered by cannibals, unless there are no missionaries on that bank.
